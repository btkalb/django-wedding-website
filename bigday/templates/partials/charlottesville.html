Charlottesville, Virginia

Getting There and Away

Charlottesville is located about in central Virginia and is home to Michelle's alma mater the University of Virginia.

For those coming from the Washington DC area, it is a 2 hour drive South from Dulles International Airport and a 2.5 hour drive South from Ronald Reagan International Airport
