Charlottesville, Virginia

Getting There

Charlottesville is in central Virginia and is home to Michelle's alma mater the University of Virginia.

For those coming from the Washington DC area, it is a 2 hour drive South from Dulles International Airport and a 2.5 hour drive South from Ronald Reagan International Airport.

Things to Do

There's no shortage of things to do in Charlottesville! Some ideas are listed below during your weekend stay.

Wineries & Breweries

If you have a designated driver, nothing beats a leisurely afternoon visiting a few of Charlottesville's beautiful vineyards and breweries.

  • Blue Mountain Brewery
  • Veritas Winery
  • Gabriele Rausse Winery
  • Pippin Hill Winery
  • King Family Winery
  • Starr Hill Brewery

Food

  • Bodo's Bagels (best bagels ever, multiple locations in Charlottesville)
  • Christian's Pizza
  • Little John's
  • Marco & Luca
  • Take It Away

Sites to See

  • Historic downtown mall features shops, restaurants
  • Monticello - Thomas Jefferson's home
  • University of Virginia - Tours are available or walk the historic lawn. There are also restaurants, bars, and some shopping next to the campus on what's known as "The Corner".
